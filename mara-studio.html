MARA STUDIO

Founded in 2017, MARA studio navigates the tension between precision and intuition — a studio where objects emerge as quiet statements rather than declarations. The owner Mariela Ganova works across wood, metal, and leather, each piece exists as a singular presence, shaped by contrast: strict geometry against soft, almost organic deformation.

Furniture, lighting, and objects exist in the in-between — where defined form dissolves into fluid transition, and clarity gives way to ambiguity. The design is a tangible manifestation of a captured feeling.
